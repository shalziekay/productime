Study Tips

1. Underline key points of what you are reading

2. Take notes to easily remember the main ideas

3. Use a Mind Map to brainstorm ideas, outlining, and exam prep

4. Use flashcards to memorize different facts, dates, formulas, or vocabulary

5. Study case studies to understand a theory

6. Take practice quizzes or exams to know where your strengths and weaknesses are

7. Brainstorm to expand every possible idea out of any topic

8. Create or use mnemonics when memorizing lists and sets

9. Organize your study time by making a study timetable

10. Draw ideas and concepts so you can better memorize concepts by associating pictures with them
